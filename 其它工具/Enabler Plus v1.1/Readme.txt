Introduction
------------

Enabler Plus allows the user to enable disabled Windows, Menu & Controls such as buttons, static, editbox & toolbar items.



How To Use
-------------

Unzip & run EnablerPlus.exe, Left click on the Enabler Plus tray icon to On/Off, Right click to show Enabler Plus Menu.

When Enabler Plus is running all disabled menu will be automatic enable & just click on the disabled window/control to make it enable.
